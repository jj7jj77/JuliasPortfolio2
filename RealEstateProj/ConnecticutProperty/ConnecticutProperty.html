House Description

Perched on a picture-perfect cul-de-sac with your own backyard access to Saugatuck Valley Trail of 14 miles, an apple orchard, and blueberry bushes. This Orchard Cottege has a contemporary style with 5-bedroom home on 2 acres that will take your breath away.

An indoor heated pool, with vaulted beamed ceiling opens to the patio. The indoor pool with 10 sliders-surround transitions to a "covered outdoor pool" in the summer. Two story foyer and living room with walls of glass opens to huge deck and private flat yard with spectacular views of dual waterfalls and Peaceful Pond that could be used as mini ice-skating rink in the winter. Sauna, gym, audio system speakers throughout home, radiant heated floor in foyer and guest bath, home office, two fireplaces (one double-sided), central vacuum system, wine cellar, cedar closet, finished basement, four garage bays, and smart-home wifi upgrades throughout. A lot of storage, bonus space above the garage.
