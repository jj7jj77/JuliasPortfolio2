House Description

This stunning Tudor Oakhall Cottege was built in 1989 on a lot of 3+ acres. It is a Single Family home with 2,606 sqft on a 1.1 acre lot with 2 garages.

This house has some secret passageways throughout the house. Inside the house, there is a country kitchen with an antique electric stove, updated appliances. The eating nook that looks out to the pool/patio area. The room next has a formal dining, looking out to the pool/patio area, as well. The last room, the living room with a fireplace and French doors leading to the backyard. The Master suite with a walk-in shower, bath, walk-in closet, and fireplace. The other 3 bedrooms have single occupant bathrooms. Downstairs there is a basement with a bar.

Outside there is a 38' pool with waterfall and detached slide. Along with the pool there is hottub spa and sauna. On the outskirts of the pool there is a gazebo, lawn, palms, a pool house, and playground with a trampoline. The lawn is perimited with lined trees, fully-fenced, and garage with epoxy flooring.
