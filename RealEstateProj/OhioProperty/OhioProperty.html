House Description

This sprawling Willow Ranch home offers an open concept and exclusive living areas. A welcoming entry passes by a multi-use room that can be used as a den or a dining room. The spacious great room is showcased with gorgeous wide-planked cherry hardwood, an intimate gas fireplace, a soaring vaulted ceiling and is open to the kitchen featuring cherry cabinets, gas range, stainless steel appliances, granite counters. All overlooking a bright sunroom. A split floor plan, w/ the Master Suite on one end & two large bedrooms on the other. The finished walk-up basement is perfect for guests and has its own mini kitchen, bedroom, full bath, office, hobby & storage space. This house awaits in the sunny & private backyard w/ an inground swimming pool. A 3-car garage, charming shed.
