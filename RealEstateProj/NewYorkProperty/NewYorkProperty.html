House Description

You can live feets away from a 150 ft lake frontage with a spacious, custom built dock designed for all of your summertime fun. This Lakecrest Cottege has breathtaking panoramic lake views. A full wrap around porch allows for you to continue to enjoy the lake many months of the year. Upon entering the home you are welcomed into a large living room and recreational room which leads to large kitchen with a stone island and porch access. There is a dining room and a bonus room that could be used a home office or an additional bedroom. Off the dining room is a secluded patio area just waiting for a hot tub! 4-5 bedrooms, 2 ½ bathrooms, first floor laundry and a 3 car attached garage with a bedroom/living space above are just some of what this beauty offers.
